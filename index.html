Welcome to Mabog National High School

Mabog National High School prides itself on the quality of its educational programmes, the Professionalism of each Teachers and staff, the enthusiam of its Student and the high level of support provided by the parents. This is a public school and actively seek participation and involvement from the whole school community. Mabog National High School was founded in the year of 2003. The School has become one of the most transformed and has gained recognition in both extra and co-curricular activities. Over the years, the school has continued to provide education and assessing the learner’s to improve students performance.
